Research

My goal is to build intelligent agents that can efficiently generalize to solve a diverse set of tasks. To this end, I am interested in techniques from reinforcement learning, machine learning, computer vision and cognitive psychology. In the past, I have been interested in Extreme Classification and neuro-symbolic AI.
